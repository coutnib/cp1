Where Can You Cut Back?

Want to cut back on your plastic use, but not sure where to start? We've put together some ideas:

  • Bags: take your own when you go grocery shopping. This requires a little planning ahead, but will make a massive difference for this environment. Just remember to wash them! You can take larger bags for carrying groceries to your car and small mesh bags to replace produce bags.
  • Shopping: Buy at bulk stores. Many stores, like Winco or Sprouts, don't prepackage all of their produce in plastic. This makes using thos reusable bags much more effective.
  • Eating: instead of buying that bottled water, invest in a pitcher with filter and use a reusable bottle. You can also cut back on plastic utensil waste by taking your own reusables
  • Packaging: Some companies have started offering shipping options that don't use plastic. Look for it the next time you order off Amazon.
  • Toiletries/Cleaning Supplies: invest in reusable razors. Some stores are also starting to offer wholesale options for cleaners and shampoos.

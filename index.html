Going Plastic Free

It's no secret plastic is taking over the world. Unfortunately, it's taking over in the evil overlord sort of way. Not only are there now actual islands made of trashed plastic floating in our Oceans, but some studies have shown there are also plastic particles in our drinking water and in our food. It is killing birds and marine animals and is not biodegradable. The goal of PF is to help you learn how you can help with this devestating problem.


While it may be difficult to completely cut plastics out of your life, there are many options available that can help you drastically reduce your plastic footprint without going full granola. Feel free to check out our ideas page to see what you can do now to start cutting back.
